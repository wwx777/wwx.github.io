About

I am Wanxing Wu (吴宛幸) (CV), an M.S. student in Computer Science at Institut Polytechnique de Paris (IP Paris). I received my B.S. in Data Science and Big Data Technology from the Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). I am currently a Research Assistant at SUSTech-NLP, advised by Prof. Guanhua Chen.

My research interests broadly lie in efficient LLM systems and agentic AI. I have worked on LLM–SLM model collaboration for efficient routing and inference, and I am currently working on efficient agentic exploration for test-time adaptation. I am also interested in LLM for health, as an application domain where low-resource constraints and trustworthiness are both critical.
