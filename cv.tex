% ── ABOUT ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
\section{About}

I am an M.S.\ student in Computer Science at
\textbf{Institut Polytechnique de Paris (IP Paris)},
with a B.S.\ in Data Science and Big Data Technology from
\textbf{Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)}.
I am currently a Research Assistant at \textbf{SUSTech-NLP}, advised by Prof.~Guanhua Chen.

\vspace{5pt}
My research interests broadly lie in \textbf{efficient LLM systems} and \textbf{agentic AI}.
I have worked on \textbf{LLM--SLM model collaboration} for efficient routing and inference,
and I am currently working on \textbf{efficient agentic exploration} for test-time adaptation.
I am also interested in \textbf{LLM for health}, as an application domain where
low-resource constraints and trustworthiness are both critical.
